Urban cooling corridor construction planning for Freetown, Sierra Leone

This project aims to design a cooling corridor system in Freetown, Sierra Leone that maximizes cooling benefits of urban trees at lowest cost. It connects existing regions of maximum heat exposure, dense regions of urban amenities, socio-economic activities and past planting sites from the city's planting campaign. The corridor is designed to follow the street systems of Freetown, with minor mismatches due to lack of geospatial and remote sensing data in the city.
